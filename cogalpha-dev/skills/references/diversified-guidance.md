# Diversified Guidance

CogAlpha may ask a domain skill to generate candidates under one of five guidance modes.

## Modes

- `light`: minimal rewording of the assigned focus; preserve the core idea.
- `moderate`: natural variation and mild enrichment.
- `creative`: add research-oriented nuance while staying inside the assigned focus.
- `divergent`: explore a related sub-mechanism that broadens the assigned focus.
- `concrete`: translate the idea into measurable ratios, windows, or transformations.

Use the requested mode to vary reasoning, but never violate the alpha factor contract.
